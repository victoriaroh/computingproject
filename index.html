    Olympics Data Analysis

    Project Topic Choice and Exploration Questions

    Question 1: Is there a correlation between host nation and winning more? Question 2: What sports have the shortest athletes? Question 3: Which sports have the tallest athletes? Question 4: Heaviest/lighest sports? Question 5: Do countries in colder areas win more than warmer countries in the Winter Olympics, vice versa for summer? Question 6: Which teams have improved the most in terms of wins over time, and which have declined the most? Question 7: Which teams perform best in newly added sports?
